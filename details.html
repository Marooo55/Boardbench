Evaluation

Advantages & Disadvantages

✅ Advantages

  • + Extremely beginner-friendly with massive community support
  • + Huge library ecosystem — thousands of ready-made libraries
  • + Shield-compatible (stackable expansion boards)
  • + Open-source hardware and software — fully documented
  • + Robust 5V logic — easy to interface with sensors and motors
  • + Cross-platform IDE (Windows, Mac, Linux)
  • + Affordable and widely available worldwide

❌ Disadvantages

  • − Limited memory (32 KB flash, 2 KB SRAM)
  • − Slow 16 MHz clock — not suitable for heavy computation
  • − No built-in Wi-Fi or Bluetooth connectivity
  • − Relatively large form factor compared to Nano/Micro
  • − Cannot run an operating system
  • − Single-threaded — cannot multitask natively
